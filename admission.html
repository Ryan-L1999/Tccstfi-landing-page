Your Journey Starts Here

We welcome aspiring students from all backgrounds. Our admission process is straightforward, transparent, and student-friendly. Start your application today!

Requirements
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Original Card/Form 138 (Grade 12 Report Card)
  • 4 pcs of Passport size Picture
  • Phtotocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Honorable Dismissal
  • Transcript of Records
Application Steps
01
Create an Account
Register at our enrollment portal and fill out the online application form completely.
02
Take the Admission Test
Schedule and complete the Brightfield Admission Test (BAT) — online or on-campus.
03
Submit Documents
Upload or personally submit all required documents to the Registrar's Office.
04
Receive Acceptance & Enroll
Upon approval, pay the enrollment fee and complete your official enrollment.
