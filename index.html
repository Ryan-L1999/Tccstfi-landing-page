Our Guiding Principles
🔭
Vision
"Leader in producing highly qualified professionals, multi-skilled, service-oriented with irrefutable ethical and moral standards for the global society."
🎯
Mission
"As a premier non-sectarian technology educational institution, Tagum City College of Science and Technology Foundation, Inc., is committed to provide a high quality educational environment, relevant academic program; capability-building service, facilities and activities that promte strong moral values, Peace and Prosperity."
⚖️
Core Values
"Excellence · Integrity · Service · Innovation · Community"
🌟
Goals
To achieve its mission, TCCSTFI aims to:
1. Provide basic technical and college education for the total development of individual student with skill in all levels;
2. Provide the students with skills, knowledge and values for honest and gainful work;
3. Cultivate a sense of sommitment among students to be catalyst in uplifting the socio-economic well being of the community where they live in;
4. Train research-oriented students on techniques and scientific ways of finding solutions to social problems.
5. Provide outreach programs that facilitate the transfer of technology and promote self-reliance among the less-privileged of the Philippine society;
6. Produce highly competent grauates with strong leadership and a high sense of moral and ethical standards for the industry needs;
7. Develop and strengthen the values of leadership, cooperation, collegiality and respect for human dignity in a culture of peace.

Admission & Enrollment

Your journey to excellence starts here. Apply online or visit us — we'd love to welcome you to TCCSTFI.

Home › Admission

Your Journey Starts Here

We welcome aspiring students from all backgrounds. Our admission process is straightforward, transparent, and student-friendly. Start your application today!

Requirements
  • Original & photocopy of Form 138 (Grade 12 Report Card)
  • PSA-issued Birth Certificate (2 copies)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2×2 ID Photos — 4 copies, white background
  • Medical Certificate from a licensed physician
  • Passed the Brightfield Admission Test (BAT)
  • Senior High School Diploma or equivalent
Application Steps
01
Create an Account
Register at our enrollment portal and fill out the online application form completely.
02
Take the Admission Test
Schedule and complete the Brightfield Admission Test (BAT) — online or on-campus.
03
Submit Documents
Upload or personally submit all required documents to the Registrar's Office.
04
Receive Acceptance & Enroll
Upon approval, pay the enrollment fee and complete your official enrollment.
