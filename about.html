Our History

A Legacy of Learning & Leadership

Tagum City College of Science and Technology Inc. was born from a dream to bring quality education to the community — a vision that has grown into one of the region's most trusted academic institutions.

2007
Founding
The institution was founded by spouses Deo and Leodivica Casintahan as Tagum City Institute of Technology, operating as a technical-vocational school on second floor of the Pereyras Building.
2011
Expanding and Rebranding to Tagum City College of Science and Technology
The school grew into a larger institution rebranding as the Tagum City College of Science and Technology, Inc (TCCSTI), and moved to a one hectare site on Gazmen Road..
2026
Current Status
As of 2026 TCCSTFI offering a technical-vocational and college level programs with focus on workforce development.
